About Us

Welcome to Libera Tour India, your ultimate travel partner in discovering the breathtaking diversity and rich cultural heritage of India. Established with a passion for exploration and a deep love for India’s traditions, we are dedicated to creating memorable and immersive travel experiences tailored just for you.

Whether you dream of watching the sunrise over the Taj Mahal, experiencing the spiritual aura of Varanasi, exploring the desert forts of Rajasthan, or relaxing on the beaches of Goa — we make it all possible with professional planning and personalized service. Our mission is to offer authentic journeys that connect travelers with the heart of India.

Travel Experience

At Libera Tour India, we believe that travel is not just about destinations — it's about the experiences you carry with you. Our curated tours are designed to engage all your senses. Imagine sipping masala chai in the Himalayas, attending a vibrant Holi festival in Mathura, or taking a peaceful boat ride on Kerala’s backwaters.

With local guides, handpicked stays, and insider access to cultural events, we ensure every journey is a deep dive into India's beauty and complexity. Whether you're a solo adventurer, a couple on a honeymoon, or a family seeking adventure, we tailor each experience to your interests and pace.

Itineraries

Our flexible itineraries are crafted for every kind of traveler:

  • Golden Triangle Tour (Delhi – Agra – Jaipur): Perfect for first-timers who want to experience India's architectural marvels, including the Taj Mahal and Amber Fort.
  • Spiritual Varanasi & Khajuraho: Dive into ancient traditions, river rituals, and stunning temple art.
  • Rajasthan Heritage Safari: Explore desert cities like Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, and Udaipur with royal palace stays.
  • Southern Delights: Discover the temples of Tamil Nadu, the beaches of Kerala, and the lush Western Ghats.
  • Himalayan Adventure: Trek in Himachal or experience peace in a Ladakh monastery retreat.

Our team is happy to customize routes based on your travel dates, budget, and special interests.
